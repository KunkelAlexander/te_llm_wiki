## Summary

**Key quantitative findings:**
- The scraped fulltext for this page is only a short excerpt/abstract; the full quantitative analysis is captured in the companion joint letter below.

- This July 2022 T&E note previews the same argument made jointly with easyJet ahead of the 7 July 2022 European Parliament vote on ReFuelEU Aviation: that the TRAN committee's widened SAF definition (adding recycled carbon fuels and a broader biofuel feedstock base) risks flooding the EU aviation market with fuels linked to deforestation and higher food prices.
- Calls for MEPs to revert to the European Commission's stricter original definition, limited to Annex IX feedstocks of the Renewable Energy Directive.
