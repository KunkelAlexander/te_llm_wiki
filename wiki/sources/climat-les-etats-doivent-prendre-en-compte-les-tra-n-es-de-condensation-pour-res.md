# Climate: states must take contrails into account to comply with the Paris Agreement, new legal analysis finds

*Publication · France · 2025-08-28*

> According to the analysis, countries that do not account for aviation's so-called "non-CO2" effects are failing to meet their international climate commitments. T&E and Opportunity Green are calling on states to act before COP30 in Brazil.
