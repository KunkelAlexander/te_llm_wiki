# E-fuels as the key to Germany's competitiveness and industrial leadership

*Original title (German): "E-Fuels als Schlüssel für Deutschlands Wettbewerbsfähigkeit und industrielle Führungsrolle"*

*Open letter · Germany · 2025-09-24*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/te-deutschland/articles/e-fuels-als-schluessel-fuer-deutschlands-wettbewerbsfaehigkeit-und-industrielle-fuehrungsrolle)

*(No abstract was published for this document; summary below written from the source text.)*

Germany is a global e-fuels frontrunner — research funding, first plants and the world's first national blending target have kick-started the market ramp-up, and e-kerosene made from green hydrogen and CO2 can cut aviation emissions by more than 90%. Despite this head start, Germany's industrial ramp-up of e-kerosene is stalling, held back by high electricity prices, contradictory policy signals (including the scrapping of national power-to-liquid quotas and a €2bn funding cut), and structural market failures preventing frontrunner projects from reaching a final investment decision.
