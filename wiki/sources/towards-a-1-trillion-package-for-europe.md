# Towards a €1 trillion package for Europe

*Briefing · Brussels · 2024-04-23*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/articles/towards-a-eur1-trillion-package-for-europe) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/TE_-Investment-Plan_April-2024.pdf)

*(No abstract was published for this document; summary below written from the source text.)*

T&E proposes a Climate and Social Investment Plan to raise €1 trillion between 2025 and 2034, mostly through €850 billion in joint EU borrowing (EU Green Bonds), to close the European Commission's estimated €620 billion annual climate investment gap after the Recovery and Resilience Facility ends in 2026. The plan splits funding between a Green Industry pillar — scaling up batteries, smart grids, renewables and their mineral supply chains — and a Social and Just Transition pillar addressing energy poverty and the socioeconomic impacts of decarbonisation.
