# New analysis: more air traffic mostly does not lead to economic growth

*Original title (German): "Neue Analyse: Mehr Flugverkehr führt meist nicht zu Wirtschaftswachstum"*

*Press release · Germany · 2025-11-13*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/te-deutschland/articles/analyse-mehr-flugverkehr-fuehrt-meist-nicht-zu-wirtschaftswachstum)

> New research findings refute blanket claims from the aviation industry that justify airport expansion on the grounds that more flights benefit the economy. Frankfurt Airport is being expanded despite regional "saturation" — meaning additional flight capacity has no, or even a negative, effect on economic growth.
