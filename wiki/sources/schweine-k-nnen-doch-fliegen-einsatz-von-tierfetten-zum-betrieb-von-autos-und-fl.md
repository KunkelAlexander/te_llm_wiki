# "Pigs can fly after all": use of animal fats to power cars and planes increasingly unsustainable

*Original title (German): "'Schweine können doch fliegen': Einsatz von Tierfetten zum Betrieb von Autos und Flugzeugen immer unhaltbarer"*

*Press release · Germany · 2023-05-31*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/te-deutschland/articles/schweine-konnen-doch-fliegen-einsatz-von-tierfetten-zum-betrieb-von-autos-und-flugzeugen-immer-unhaltbarer)

> Biofuels made from waste products such as animal fats were long seen as a cure-all for emission-free transport in Europe. But a single flight from Frankfurt to New York alone requires 10,400 dead pigs. The growing use of animal fats in transport is raising increasing concerns, since their availability is limited and their labelling carries risks of fraud.
