# Decarbonising long-haul trucking in Germany

*Original title (German): "Die Dekarbonisierung des Lkw-Fernverkehrs in Deutschland"*

*Report · Germany · 2021-04-06*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/te-deutschland/articles/how-decarbonise-long-haul-trucking-germany) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/2021_04_TE_Dekarbonisierung_des_Lkw_Fernverkehrs_in_Deutschland_kurzfassung_final.pdf)

> Efficiency measures — improving conventional diesel trucks' fuel efficiency, incentivising a shift of freight to rail and inland waterways, and improving logistics efficiency — can help reduce Germany's freight-transport emissions, but are not enough on their own to meet the country's 2030 and 2050 climate targets. Trucks must be fully decarbonised by 2050 at the latest.
