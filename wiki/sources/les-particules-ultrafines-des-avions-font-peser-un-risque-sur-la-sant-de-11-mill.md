# Ultrafine particles from aircraft pose a health risk to 11 million French people

*Original title (French): "Les particules ultrafines des avions font peser un risque sur la santé de 11 millions de Français"*

*Report · France · 2024-06-25*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/te-france/articles/les-particules-ultrafines-des-avions-font-peser-un-risque-sur-la-sante-de-11-millions-de-francais) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/CEDelft_Final_Study.pdf)

> A new study conducted by Transport & Environment (T&E) indicates that thousands of cases of high blood pressure, diabetes and dementia in France and Europe could be linked to the tiny particles emitted by aircraft.
