# Spotlight on e-SAF

*News · Brussels · 2025-06-16*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/topics/planes/saf-observatory/spotlight-on-e-kerosene) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/202504_e-kerosene_report.pdf)

*(No abstract was published for this document; summary below written from the source text.)*

A T&E report (June 2025) on Europe's e-SAF/e-kerosene project pipeline: 41 large-scale projects were under development as of May 2025 (up from 27 in January 2024), giving Europe an early lead on ReFuelEU's e-kerosene sub-mandate, but no large-scale plant is yet operational anywhere in the world and none in Europe have reached a final investment decision — a gap the report warns risks ceding Europe's head start to China and the US.
