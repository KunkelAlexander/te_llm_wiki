# ETS2 - a €300bn opportunity to help transition European citizens away from fossil fuels and imported oil

*News · Brussels · 2025-06-03*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/topics/climate-instruments/ets-2) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/ETS2_implementation_report_03062025.pdf)

*(No abstract was published for this document; summary below written from the source text.)*

A T&E report (June 2025) setting out recommendations for a successful rollout of the EU's new ETS2 carbon-pricing scheme for road-transport and heating fuels, which takes effect in 2027. It argues ETS2 revenue — potentially hundreds of billions of euros EU-wide — should fund a fair transition, with at least half returned as direct financial support, so the scheme does not fall regressively on low-income households and transport-dependent small businesses.
