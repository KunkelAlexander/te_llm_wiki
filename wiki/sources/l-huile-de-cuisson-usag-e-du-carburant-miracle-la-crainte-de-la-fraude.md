# Used cooking oil: from miracle fuel to fear of fraud

*Original title (French): "L'huile de cuisson usagée, du carburant miracle à la crainte de la fraude"*

*Briefing · France · 2024-06-18*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/te-france/articles/lhuile-de-cuisson-du-carburant-miracle-a-la-crainte-de-la-fraude) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/202406_UCO_trade_briefing_final.pdf)

> As the aviation sector increasingly uses used cooking oil to decarbonise, a new analysis shows that available quantities will plateau and will be unable to meet future demand. Worse, some exporting countries such as Malaysia show suspicious export levels, raising fears of fraud with virgin palm oil and therefore a catastrophic climate impact.
