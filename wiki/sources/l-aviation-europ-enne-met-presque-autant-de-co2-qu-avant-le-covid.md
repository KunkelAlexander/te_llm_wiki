# European aviation emits almost as much CO2 as before Covid

*Original title (French): "L'aviation européenne émet presque autant de CO2 qu'avant le Covid"*

*Press release · France · 2025-04-28*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/te-france/articles/laviation-europeenne-emet-presque-autant-de-co2-quavant-le-covid) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/Emissions-aerien-et-marche-carbone-France-1.pdf)

> Last year, according to T&E, flights departing Europe to all destinations emitted almost as much CO2 as in 2019, with the same trend observed in France. The EU's carbon market (ETS) still does not account for the sector's real emissions cost.
