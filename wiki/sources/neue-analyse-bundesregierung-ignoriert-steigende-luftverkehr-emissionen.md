# New analysis: German government ignores rising aviation emissions

*Original title (German): "Neue Analyse: Bundesregierung ignoriert steigende Luftverkehr-Emissionen"*

*Press release · Germany · 2025-03-14*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/te-deutschland/articles/neue-analyse-bundesregierung-ignoriert-steigende-luftverkehr-emissionen) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/Deutsche-Luftfahrt-2024_-Emissionen-Trends-und-Marktentwicklung.pdf)

> In 2024, almost a million flights departed from German airports - 113 per hour. As a result, the sector's emissions rose by 4 percent compared to 2023. Yet they are barely accounted for by the Climate Protection Act.
