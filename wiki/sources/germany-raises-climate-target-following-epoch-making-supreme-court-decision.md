# Germany raises climate target following "epoch-making" supreme court decision

*News · Brussels · 2021-05-28*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/articles/germany-raises-climate-target-following-epoch-making-supreme-court-decision)

> "Epoch-making", "great" and "significant," tweeted Germany's economy minister Peter Altmaier, after the country's supreme court declared his own government's climate protection measures insufficient, forcing it to rewrite its climate targets. With an election on the horizon, Altmaier's CDU, alongside its coalition partner the SPD, subsequently raised the target above and beyond the court's suggestion.
