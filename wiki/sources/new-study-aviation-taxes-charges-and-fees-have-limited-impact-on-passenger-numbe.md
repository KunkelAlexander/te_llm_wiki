# New study: Aviation taxes, charges and fees have limited impact on passenger numbers

*Press release · Brussels · 2025-10-29*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/articles/new-study-aviation-taxes-charges-and-fees-have-limited-impact-on-passenger-numbers)

*(No abstract was published for this document; summary below written from the source text.)*

A study by two economists commissioned by T&E, comparing location-based costs (taxes, airport and air-navigation fees) against passenger volumes at 101 European airports in 2024, finds only a weak correlation between the two — Heathrow, for example, has both the highest location-based costs and the highest passenger numbers in Europe — refuting airline claims that national aviation taxes and fees alone are driving passengers away.
