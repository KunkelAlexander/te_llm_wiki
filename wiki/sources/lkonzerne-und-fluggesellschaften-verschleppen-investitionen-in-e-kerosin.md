# Oil companies and airlines are delaying investments in e-kerosene

*Original title (German): "Ölkonzerne und Fluggesellschaften verschleppen Investitionen in E-Kerosin"*

*Press release · Germany · 2024-12-03*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/te-deutschland/articles/oelkonzerne-und-fluggesellschaften-verschleppen-investitionen-in-e-kerosin)

> Most airlines show no serious ambition to switch to e-kerosene. Oil companies are also investing too little in sustainable aviation fuels. This not only slows the shift to climate-neutral flying but is also a missed opportunity for the German economy.
