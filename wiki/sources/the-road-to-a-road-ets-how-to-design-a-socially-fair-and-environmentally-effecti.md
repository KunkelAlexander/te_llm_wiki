# The road to a road ETS: How to design a socially fair and environmentally effective system

*Briefing · Brussels · 2021-12-15*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/articles/the-road-to-a-road-ets-how-to-design-a-socially-fair-and-environmentally-effective-system) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/Road-to-road-ETS-10-ETS2_SCF-criteria-1.pdf)

> The decarbonisation of road transport will have an impact on the whole of society and could become an economic burden on the most vulnerable. In a new briefing, Transport and Environment (T&E) proposes a set of criteria to ensure ETS2 and the SCF are socially fair and environmentally integral.
