# Now is the time for an ambitious European e-kerosene target

*Letter · Brussels · 2021-07-02*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/articles/now-is-the-time-for-an-ambitious-european-e-kerosene-target) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/2021_06_Letter_accelerate_e-kerosene_final.pdf)

> T&E, Lufthansa, the Global Alliance Powerfuels, pilots representatives, and other aviation industry and energy players have called on the European Commission to set ambitious targets for e-kerosene, a green synthetic fuel, when it proposes the world's first sustainable jet fuel law this month.
