# Weak innovation in aviation risks slowing down the sector's decarbonisation

*Original title (French): "La faible innovation dans l'aérien risque de ralentir la décarbonation de l'aviation" —
also published in English as [The aviation industry and the stall in aircraft innovation](the-aviation-industry-and-the-stall-in-aircraft-innovation.md)*

*Briefing · France · 2025-06-11*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/te-france/articles/la-faible-innovation-dans-laerien-risque-de-ralentir-la-decarbonation-de-laviation) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/The-aviation-industry-and-the-stall-in-aircraft-innovation_Aircraft-tech-briefing.pdf)

> As the aerospace sector prepares for the Paris Air Show, new T&E analysis finds aviation is relying far too heavily on conventional technology, putting its decarbonisation targets at risk. If aircraft manufacturers chose to genuinely innovate, the resulting improvements — combined with fiscal measures to manage demand — could avoid the equivalent of 62 million cars' worth of annual CO2 emissions.
