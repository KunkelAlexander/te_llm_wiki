# European aviation set to spend billions on offsetting schemes

*Briefing · Brussels · 2025-09-22*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/articles/european-aviation-spending-billions-on-offsetting-schemes) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/ICAO-General-Assembly-1.pdf)

*(No abstract was published for this document; summary below written from the source text.)*

A T&E and Carbon Market Watch analysis (September 2025, ahead of the 42nd ICAO Assembly) finding CORSIA, the UN's aviation offsetting scheme, will offset only 26% of EU aviation's CO2 emissions by 2035 at a cost of €7-43bn over 2024-2035, versus extending the EU ETS to all departing flights from 2027, which could raise an extra €147bn by 2040. It also documents industry capture of ICAO (a 14:1 industry-to-green-group ratio at meetings held under non-disclosure agreements).
