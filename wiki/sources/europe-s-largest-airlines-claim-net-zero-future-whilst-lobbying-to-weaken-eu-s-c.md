# Europe's largest airlines claim net zero future whilst lobbying to weaken EU's climate laws

*Press release · Brussels · 2022-04-07*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/articles/europes-largest-airlines-claim-net-zero-future-whilst-lobbying-to-weaken-eus-climate-laws) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/IAGs-position-on-EU-legislative-proposals-of-the-Fit-for-55.pdf)

> New analysis by Transport & Environment (T&E) finds that the four legacy carriers - Air France, Lufthansa, Iberia and Aer Lingus - are working to water down EU's climate plans for aviation, letting up to 72% of EU aviation emissions off the hook [1].
