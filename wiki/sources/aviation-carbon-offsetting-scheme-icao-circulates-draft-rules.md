# Aviation carbon offsetting scheme: ICAO circulates draft rules

*News · Brussels · 2018-01-09*

[Read the original publication](https://www.transportenvironment.org/articles/aviation-carbon-offsetting-scheme-icao-circulates-draft-rules) · [PDF](https://www.transportenvironment.org/uploads/files/2018_01_ICAO_CORSIA_draft_20SARP.pdf)

> The UN aviation agency ICAO has circulated draft rules for its carbon offsetting scheme (CORSIA). These rules were drafted after several years of technical work in ICAO. In November 2017 ICAO's 36-state Council adopted the draft rules for CORSIA and circulated it to all 192 ICAO states for their feedback. States have until 5 March 2018 to respond, after which the Council will formally adopt the rules either unchanged or with amendments. The draft rules are available to read here.

## Summary

**Key quantitative findings:**
- ICAO's 36-state Council adopted the draft CORSIA rules in November 2017 and circulated them to all 192 ICAO member states for comment.
- States had until 5 March 2018 to respond, after which the Council would formally adopt the rules (unchanged or amended).
- The proposed applicability date for the new Annex 16, Volume IV (administration and monitoring/reporting/verification requirements) was 1 January 2019; CO2 offsetting requirements and related actions were proposed to apply from 1 January 2021.
